Our Vision

A new home for horses & riders

Son Nova is more than a riding school — it's a place where passion for horses meets the tranquility of the Mallorcan landscape. Nestled in the island's heartland, our centre offers quality facilities in an atmosphere of genuine warmth and dedication.

Whether you're a young beginner taking your first steps in the saddle, a competitive rider preparing for your next tournament, or simply looking for a trusted home for your horse — you'll find it here.
